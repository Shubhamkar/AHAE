What is the difference between the Parliament and the Government?

The Parliament comprises of the Lok Sabha and the Rajya Sabha. It is the legislative body of the country.

In a strict sense of the word 'Government', it comprises of the Prime Minister and the Council of Ministers, along with the Civil Servants. The government is responsible for execution of the law.
