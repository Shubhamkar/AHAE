What is the difference between Lok Sabha and Vidhan Sabha?

Firstly, we have a nation - India. The nation is divided into several parts - known as states, for better administration purposes.

The Lok Sabha corresponds to the whole nation while the Vidhan Sabha corresponds to a state. There is a single Lok Sabha for the whole nation whereas, since there are multiple states in the nation, there are multiple Vidhan Sabha in the nation.
