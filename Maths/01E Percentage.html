Exercise on Percentage

A] Express these as a fraction of 100:

1. 0.3% 2. 3:10 3. 1
4. 0.48 5. 2/3 6. 12%

B] Which of the following represent the fraction 33/100?

1. 0.33% 2. 33 3. 33:100

C] State true or false. Why?

  1. 15% of the objects means that we know for sure that there are 100 objects.
  2. If there are 100 objects, 15% of them means 15 objects.

I will again repeat that attempt these exercises only once you know what fractions are, what ratios are, and how to change the denominators. (What are denominators?)
