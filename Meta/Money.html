Dhangyan

Dhangyan is a website dedicated for Financial Literacy. This has been made by Tata Capital, and requires a free registration to access the course. The registration is simply to maintain separate user accounts, and save progress individually for each user. It also provides an End-of-Course assessment. The course is available in both Hindi and English.

It covers the following topics:

  • Smart Goals
  • Budgeting
  • Insurance
  • Investments
  • Banking
  • Borrowing
  • Stock Market
  • Investment Options
  • Retirement

Note that the website has not been optimised for small screens - use a laptop for the website. You may try its android app.
