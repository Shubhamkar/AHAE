PS: Even a healthy heart can stop suddenly - known as sudden cardiac arrest. Death can be prevented if a CPR is performed. Some 700,000 people die due to sudden cardiac arrests [each year]. With a population of 1.4 billion, and a lifespan of 80 years, that translates to a 4% chance that you'd die due to a sudden cardiac arrest, if someone around you does not know CPR!
