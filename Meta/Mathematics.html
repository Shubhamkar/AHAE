Mathematics

We think that humans who did it before us have done a much better job of explaining mathematics, than we ever can. Therefore, we link to some of the mathematics resources available on the web:

  • Khan Academy, if your focus is succeeding at usual schools.
  • Math is fun!, which we prefer.
  • Art of Problem Solving, if you plan for a math-intensive career, as in engineering or mathematical sciences, or perhaps, pure mathematics.

The topics which we would like the student to go through (before visiting the land of Science) include:

  1. All the topics in Data
  2. All the topics in Measurement
  3. All the topics in Money
  4. All the topics in Numbers except the ones on Special Numbers, Other Number Systems, last (third) section of Other Pages
  5. The topics on area in Geometry
  6. The topics on "The Basics" in Algebra
