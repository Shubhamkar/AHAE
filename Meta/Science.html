The goal is to enable to learner to think scientifically (and perhaps, to enlighten the learner with various types of thinking even!), to illustrate the various flaws of reasoning. We are more interested in enabling the learning to carry out effective experiments (in natural or social sciences!) to test his/her own understanding of things.
