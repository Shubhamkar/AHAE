Until the age of 10 (urban/middle-upper) to 12 (rural/lower), an Indian is expected to know the
  • languages Regional, Hindi and English, to the extent of comprehension and identifiable pronunciation.
  • mathematics, as far as the student's taste allows: higher the level of maths, the better the "scientific method" can be taught in higher classes
  • general science, to improve the understanding of the surroundings and to develop better habits in the society
