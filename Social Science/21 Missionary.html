Who is a Missionary?

A missionary is a member of a religious group sent into an area to proselytize and/or perform ministries of service, such as education, literacy, social justice, health care, and economic development. The term is most commonly used for Christian missions, but can be used for any creed or ideology.

- extracted from Missionary, Wikipedia as on 25th December 2017
