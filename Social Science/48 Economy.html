What is Economy?

Economy is an abstract thing.

It refers to all the sets of activities related to the production, transport and consumption of goods and services.

Thus, the economy of a country refers to the activities concerned with the production, transport and consumption of goods and services occuring in that country.

Economic growth refers to the increase in the productive activities.
